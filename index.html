Welcome!

My name is Viktoria Haghani. I typically go by Viki, and my pronouns are she/her(s). I am a fourth-year PhD Candidate in the Integrative Genetics and Genomics Graduate Group at UC Davis. I am co-mentored by Dr. Janine LaSalle and Dr. Ian Korf, where my primary focus is applying bioinformatics tools to large sequencing data sets to answer biological questions. I really enjoy automating data analysis pipelines and writing scripts. Outside of work, I enjoy cooking, baking, camping, doing puzzles, painting, playing video games, and lounging with my pet iguana, Stella, and leopard gecko, Zoscia.
